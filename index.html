About the Game

In Colour Falls, colored balls fall from the top of the screen, and your goal is to tap only the balls that match the border color. The game gets faster over time, testing your reflexes and focus. Tap the wrong color or let the correct ones fall through, and you lose a life. Watch out for heart power-ups to regain lives and points boosts for bonus points! Can you top the leaderboard?
