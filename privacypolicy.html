Privacy Policy for Colour Falls

Last updated: 11th October 2024 1. Introduction Welcome to Colour Falls. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our game. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. By playing Colour Falls, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. 2. Information We Collect We do not collect personal information directly. However, the following information may be collected and used by third-party services integrated into the game: a. Google AdMob We use Google AdMob to serve ads in Colour Falls. AdMob may collect certain information about your device, such as your device ID, IP address, and information about your interactions with ads. For more information on what data Google collects and how it uses it, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy. b. Locally Stored Data (Leaderboard Scores) Colour Falls stores your high scores locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data is not sent to any server or shared with third parties. The game saves up to the top 5 highest scores you achieve, and this information is stored solely for your personal use. c. Score Sharing Colour Falls allows you to share your score using a share sheet. When you choose to share your score, the text containing your score is generated, and you may select an app (such as social media or messaging) to share it. When you share your score, the app or service you use to share may collect information in accordance with its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party apps you use to share scores. 3. How We Use Your Information The game uses the data collected by Google AdMob to: Serve relevant advertisements to users. Improve the performance of ads within the game. Leaderboard scores are stored locally on your device and are used solely for the purpose of displaying your high scores in the game. 4. Third-Party Services Colour Falls uses the following third-party services that may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies: Google AdMob: For displaying ads. For more information, please visit Google’s Privacy Policy. When you share your score using social media or messaging apps, those services have their own privacy policies which govern the data they collect. Please review their policies for more details. 5. Data Retention Leaderboard Scores: The leaderboard scores are stored locally on your device and are not shared with any external parties. You may delete the game or clear the app’s data to remove your scores from your device. Google AdMob: For details on data retention policies related to ads, please review Google AdMob’s privacy policy. 6. Children's Privacy Colour Falls is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it as soon as possible. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our privacy policy from time to time. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes. Updates to the privacy policy will be posted on this page. This policy is effective as of 11th October 2024. 8.
