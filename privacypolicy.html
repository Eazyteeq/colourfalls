Privacy Policy for Colour Falls

Last updated: 28th October 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Colour Falls. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our game. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

By playing Colour Falls, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information. However, the following information may be collected and used by third-party services integrated into the game:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to serve ads in Colour Falls. AdMob may collect certain information about your device, such as your device ID, IP address, and information about your interactions with ads. For more information, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Locally Stored Data (Leaderboard Scores): Colour Falls stores your high scores locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data is not sent to any server or shared with third parties. The game saves up to the top 5 highest scores you achieve, and this information is stored solely for your personal use.
  • Score Sharing: Colour Falls allows you to share your score using a share sheet. When you choose to share your score, the text containing your score is generated, and you may select an app (such as social media or messaging) to share it. When you share your score, the app or service you use may collect information in accordance with its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party apps you use to share scores.

3. How We Use Your Information

The game may use data collected by Google AdMob to:

  • Serve relevant advertisements to users.
  • Improve the performance of ads within the game.

Leaderboard scores are stored locally on your device and are used solely for the purpose of displaying your high scores in the game.

4. Third-Party Services

Colour Falls uses the following third-party services that may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying ads. For more information, please visit Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • When you share your score using social media or messaging apps, those services have their own privacy policies that govern the data they collect. Please review their policies for more details.

5. Data Retention

Leaderboard Scores: The leaderboard scores are stored locally on your device and are not shared with any external parties. You may delete the game or clear the app’s data to remove your scores from your device.

Google AdMob: For details on data retention policies related to ads, please review Google AdMob’s privacy policy.

6. Children’s Privacy and User Responsibility

Colour Falls is designed to be enjoyed by users of any age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users, regardless of age, and recommend that children play the game under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

By playing Colour Falls, users accept all responsibility for their use of the game, including any interactions or consequences arising from gameplay. The creators of Colour Falls disclaim all liability to the fullest extent permitted by law for any issues arising from the use or misuse of the game.

If you are a parent or guardian and have any concerns about your child’s use of the game, please feel free to contact us. We are committed to addressing any issues that may arise promptly and responsibly.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes. Updates to the privacy policy will be posted on this page.

This policy is effective as of 11th October 2024.
